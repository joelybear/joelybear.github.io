So many ways to make a difference...

There are many ways in which you can support the important work of The Joely Bear Appeal, either by raising money for us or lending a hand in other ways. The charity is entirely run by volunteers and incurs virtually no running costs - any funds raised go directly towards our charitable projects.

Here are some ways in which you can support The Joely Bear Appeal:

Fundraise

Got a great idea for a fundraising event? Perhaps you're running a marathon? Nominate Joely Bear as your chosen charity and help fund our hospital projects!

Set up your Fundraising Page

Volunteer

Become a Joely Bear volunteer and help us to run our unique blood donor sessions. Lend us an hour of your time and help in the crèche, car park or refreshments areas!

Get In Touch!

Give Blood

Help us to Save Lives in the most direct way possible. Come along and give the gift of life at our next community blood donor session. Book your appointment now!

Collection Boxes

Encourage your customers to donate their spare change to Joely Bear. A full collection box can raise up to £300 to support our charitable projects - every penny counts!

Get In Touch!

Make a Donation

Consider making a donation to Joely Bear. The charity is entirely run by volunteers and incurs virtually no running costs; 100% of your donation will go towards our community projects.

Donate Online

Social Media

We're on Facebook, Twitter and Youtube. Share, like and retweet our content and spread the word about our important life-saving work! Just one click could persuade someone else to save a life.
